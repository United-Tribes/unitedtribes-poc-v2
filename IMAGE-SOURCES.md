## How to Add Real Images

1. **Option A: Purchase from Getty Images**
   - License high-res images for web use
   - Download at 1200x400px for hero images
   - Save to `/public/images/profiles/`

2. **Option B: Use Press Photos**
   - Contact Apple TV+ press department for official stills
   - AMC Networks for Better Call Saul photos
   - Usually provided for editorial/promotional use

3. **Option C: Creative Commons**
   - Search Wikimedia Commons
   - Check Flickr with Creative Commons licenses
   - Always verify attribution requirements

4. **Option D: Screen Captures**
   - Use official trailers and behind-the-scenes content
   - Apple TV+ promotional materials
   - YouTube channel official uploads
